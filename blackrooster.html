Boy With a Black Rooster (2021)
Stefanie Vor Schulte

“Cormac McCarthy meets the Fable game franchise”

A wonderfully odd little gem following a pure-hearted boy trying to fulfil his destiny amidst an immoral landscape, with only a pure-black rooster as his companion.

Katie says: As creepy as it is humorous, this is a great take on the morality story - I found it unpredictable and down-right weird. It was a big hit in Germany, and I can’t wait to see it take off here.
