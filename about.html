About & FAQs:

I love a reading setup, and chatting to my friends I realised that many people feel the same. I associate many of my favourite books with what I was doing when I was reading them and, I'll be honest, I've spent a lot of time reading in cafes. Book / Brew / Banger is predominantly a book recommendation website, but with an added emphasis on the reading experience. That's why I've decided to pair each of my book recs with a tasty coffee and a the perfect soundtrack accompaniment, which hopefully creates the ideal reading environment.

Browse through the catalogue, see which book takes your fancy. Each book has its own coffee rec and soundtrack accompaniment. Simple as that!

I'm Katie! I'm a bookseller who studied Music and had a brief dapple in the coffee industry. I also spend most of my free time reading in cafes, so I feel like I'm well-versed in reading vibes

The website is a project for a CodeFirstGirls course - I'm a total beginner so go easy on me!
